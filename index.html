Our CGPA Calculator is designed with simplicity and accuracy in mind, offering an intuitive user interface for easy navigation.

How to Calculate CGPA

Calculating your CGPA is straightforward with our tool. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to calculate CGPA:

  1. Enter each course's grade and credit hours for the semester.
  2. Add up the total grade points earned by multiplying the grade points by the credit hours for each course.
  3. Divide the total grade points by the total credit hours to get your GPA for the semester.
  4. Average the semester GPAs to find your cumulative GPA.

How to Calculate CGPA from GPA

If you’re wondering how to calculate CGPA from GPA, follow these steps:

  1. Calculate GPA for each semester using our GPA Calculator.
  2. Average the semester GPAs to determine your CGPA.

How to Calculate CGPA Out of 4

Many institutions use a scale out of 4.0 to calculate CGPA. Here’s how to calculate CGPA out of 4:

  1. Determine your GPA using the method described above.
  2. Convert your GPA to a 4.0 scale by multiplying your GPA by 4 and dividing by the maximum GPA of your scale (e.g., if your GPA is on a scale of 10, convert it to a 4-point scale by multiplying by 4 and dividing by 10).

How to Convert CGPA to Percentage

CGPA to Percentage Calculator tools can help you convert your CGPA into a percentage. Generally, the conversion formula is:

Percentage = (CGPA - 0.75) × 10

For instance, if your CGPA is 7.5, your percentage would be:

(7.5 - 0.75) × 10 = 67.5%
